## 1. The Core Insight

A CEO does not search for "AI agents" or "multi-agent orchestration." They
search for a way to stop the bleeding — in their operations, their culture,
their decision speed, or their personal bandwidth.

Business Infinity succeeds when it meets a leader at the exact moment they
type a desperate, specific query into Google or an AI assistant and find
that the Autonomous Boardroom is the answer they didn't know existed.

The pain is always one of these:
- **Cognitive**: "I can't think anymore."
- **Structural**: "My company has outgrown my brain."
- **Existential**: "If I leave, the vision dies."
