The boardroom debate isn’t a binary “go/no‑go” vote. It’s a decision tree process, where each CXO, driven by events, applies their domain leadership to propose pathways. These pathways are then evaluated through resonance scoring against the company’s purpose, and the boardroom synthesizes them into one autonomous action.  

---

🌐 Decision Tree Debate
- Event Trigger: A business system or market event occurs (ERP, CRM, MES, LinkedIn, regulatory change, competitor move, etc.).  
- Spontaneous Leadership: The relevant CXOs step forward, each proposing a pathway rooted in their domain expertise and the company’s purpose.  
- Decision Tree Formation:  
  - Jobs (Visionary) → proposes a narrative pathway.  
  - Buffett (Financial) → proposes a resource allocation pathway.  
  - Deming (Operational) → proposes a workflow pathway.  
  - Ogilvy (Market) → proposes a communication pathway.  
  - Drucker (Human) → proposes a people‑centric pathway.  
- Debate: CXOs challenge each other’s branches of the tree, refining them.  
- Resonance Scoring: Each branch is scored for alignment with the company’s purpose.  
- Boardroom Convergence: The boardroom synthesizes scores into an overarching resonance decision — selecting the branch that most fully embodies the company’s purpose.  
- Autonomous Execution: The chosen pathway is enacted across systems instantly.  

---

⚡ Example: Market + CRM Event
Event: Competitor launches a flashy campaign (market), while CRM shows churn rising.  

- Ogilvy (CMO): Pathway → retention campaign, resonance score 0.9.  
- Buffett (CFO): Pathway → financial incentives, resonance score 0.7.  
- Jobs (CEO): Pathway → narrative reframing, resonance score 0.85.  
- Deming (COO): Pathway → workflow tweaks, resonance score 0.75.  
- Drucker (CHRO): Pathway → staff engagement, resonance score 0.8.  

Debate:  
- Buffett challenges Ogilvy: “Retention must not erode margins.”  
- Ogilvy counters: “Without retention, margins collapse anyway.”  
- Jobs reframes: “Both must serve the purpose of reliability, not just numbers.”  
- Deming adds: “Operational tweaks reduce churn pressure.”  
- Drucker grounds: “Staff must carry this purpose authentically.”  

Boardroom Resonance Score: Synthesized at 0.85 → retention + narrative reframing chosen.  
Autonomous Execution: CRM launches retention, LinkedIn reframes messaging, ERP adjusts workflows, HR initiates engagement.
